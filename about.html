What we do

We work towards the goal of universal safety with the hope of offering AID

A

Advocating

We raise awareness and shed light on the grave issue of gun violence through media campaigns, press, events, forums, and public speeches.

I

Informing

Most Americans take an extreme stance regarding gun violence, either no guns or more guns, disregarding the key causes. We wish to shed light on how we can systematically approach its betterment through community service and scientific approaches. We work with school districts to inform kids on gun violence and what action they can take!

D

Demonstration

We’re all about action. Through a network of communities, companies, and motivated individuals, we hope to aid in the development of technologies improving citizens’ safeties while preventing a large amount of gun deaths.

Mission Statement

Our goal is to recognize America’s ongoing issues with gun violence and work towards improving citizens’ safety through a non-partisan, systematic approach.

Our Story

We are a group of students who regard gun violence as a premier, appalling issue and wish to aid in its prevention through a non-partisan approach promoting advanced methods upholding the principles of safety while diminishing the impact of guns.

With the current political climate, there is much disarray when it comes to amending gun violence. Democrats and Republicans continue to wrangle on their respective political stages on how to amend this issue while guns continue their subjugation on American Society. Approximately 30,000 American citizens die due to firearm related deaths and 70,000 are injured yearly, prompting some lawmakers to promote gun-control legislation. At the same time, there is much reluctance to adhere to gun-restricting laws as many firearm-holding citizens defend themselves with a platform based on responsibility and are not willing to give up their guns due to concerns about their safety. This conflict is fueled by powerhouses

such as the NRA, who wield their colossal influence whenever public sentiment towards guns turns sour, making it seem impossible for a future in which there isn’t an average of one school shooting a week.

As a group of passionate students, we are appalled by this reality and know we have to take a stance. We wish to approach the issue of gun violence by promoting safety and, at the same time, the diminishment of firearms. Many companies and institutions are working towards developing technology, such as non-lethal weapons, to aid in this mission and we hope to work with them in promoting safety. Through this approach, both sides of the aisle may be appealed as citizens can make up for a deficiency in safety through advanced technology and that technology could lead to fewer gun deaths. (I WANT THIS SENTENCE TO BE bolded) - We are neither Republicans nor Democrats in our mission, just Americans who wish to make a change.
