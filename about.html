About Us

We work towards the goal of universal safety with the hope of offering AID

A

Advocating

We raise awareness and shed light on the grave issue of gun violence through media campaigns, press, events, forums, and public speeches.

I

Informing

Most Americans take an extreme stance regarding gun violence, either no guns or more guns, disregarding the key causes. We wish to shed light on how we can systematically approach its betterment through community service and scientific approaches. We work with school districts to inform kids on gun violence and what action they can take!

D

Demonstration

We’re all about action. Through a network of communities, companies, and motivated individuals, we hope to aid in the development of technologies improving citizens’ safeties while preventing a large amount of gun deaths.

Our Story

  • We have a problem

    With the current political climate, there is much disarray when it comes to amending gun violence. Approximately 30,000 American citizens die due to firearm related deaths and 70,000 are injured yearly.

  • Unsuccessful Gun Control

    The NRA wields their colossal influence to subjugate any gun-control legislation, defeating over 100 proposed gun control bills since 2011. At the same time, many firearm-holding citizens are reluctant to adhere to gun control laws due to concerns about their safety. How can we keep American citizens satisfied with their quality of safety while decreasing the amount of gun deaths?

  • Who we are

    Our Safety takes a scientific, non-partisan approach to promote effective safety throughout America. Through the right combination of informing, advocating, and action, we hope to arrive at a solution which diminishes gun violence while encouraging safety.

  • What we do

    Sponsoring and partnering with companies and institutions working towards developing safer weapon technology, we aid in the scientific movement to promote safety while keeping gun deaths at a low. We also raise awareness and explore the many facets of this complex issue through the development of a blog, “You Probably Didn’t Know”.

  • You can make a change

    It takes a lot of action and compassion to tackle this dilemma. We hope you can help us in any way possible and make America safer.
